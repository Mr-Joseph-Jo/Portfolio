Websites You Can Use Crypto Currency on To Buy Things

Cryptocurrency is no longer a niche tech obsession — it’s now a full-blown revolution that’s reshaping how we think about finance, privacy, and ownership. From investing and gaming to shopping and services, crypto is increasingly accepted across the globe.

In this post, we explore a curated list of legitimate websites where you can spend your crypto, complete with insights and direct links to get you started. Whether you’re a seasoned HODLer or a curious newcomer, these platforms can help you put your coins to real-world use.

🛒 Best Websites That Accept Cryptocurrency

1. Coingate — Crypto Checkout for E-Commerce

Coingate offers merchants and customers the ability to accept and use Bitcoin, Ethereum, and 70+ other cryptocurrencies for real goods and services. Ideal for business owners, it integrates seamlessly with platforms like Shopify, WooCommerce, and Magento.

Notable features: real-time exchange rates, fraud prevention, and invoice management. Plus, payouts in EUR, USD, or BTC make it flexible for any region.

2. CoinsBee — Global Gift Cards with Crypto

CoinsBee bridges the crypto-world and retail by offering thousands of gift cards from brands like Amazon, Netflix, Spotify, Steam, and even gaming credits — all purchasable with Bitcoin and other altcoins.

Serving users in 165+ countries, it’s a one-stop shop for topping up mobile phones, paying bills, or gifting crypto-based rewards. Their smooth checkout process supports over 100 cryptocurrencies.

3. Bitrefill — Live on Crypto

Bitrefill takes convenience to the next level. Need to pay your Netflix bill? Book a flight? Refill your mobile data? All possible with crypto using Bitrefill. It even supports the Lightning Network for ultra-fast transactions.

It’s built for global users and lets you maintain your digital life without ever converting back to fiat. Perfect for digital nomads and crypto-first users.

4. Travala — Book Hotels & Flights with Crypto

Travala is a blockchain-based travel booking platform that offers over 3 million travel products including hotels, flights, and activities — all payable in crypto.

With loyalty rewards, price matching, and support for 90+ cryptocurrencies, it’s a serious contender to traditional platforms like Expedia and Booking.com.

5. CryptoRefills — Mobile Top-Ups & Utilities

CryptoRefills helps you pay for mobile data, gift cards, and even utility bills using crypto in dozens of countries. If you’re in a region with limited banking options, this can be a game-changer.

They support payments via BTC, ETH, LTC, and even stablecoins like USDT. Plus, the onboarding process is super easy — no KYC needed for most purchases.

🔗 Why These Sites Matter for Crypto Adoption

The future of finance is decentralized — and websites like these make it easier to move away from legacy systems. Whether you’re shopping, traveling, or gifting, there’s no need to convert crypto to fiat. Just pay and go.

💬 FAQs

Q: Are these sites safe to use?
Yes, all platforms listed here are verified, reputable, and have positive community feedback. Always double-check URLs and enable two-factor authentication where available.

Q: Can I use stablecoins like USDT or USDC?
Absolutely. Most of these services support stablecoins as well as traditional cryptocurrencies.

Q: What if my country is not supported?
Check each site’s Terms of Service — some platforms have geographic restrictions due to regulations. VPNs may help in some cases, but use them wisely.
